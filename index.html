About Me

In the heart of Ethiopia, where the ancient meets the modern, there exists a 21-year-old soul deeply immersed in the fascinating world of computer science. As I embark on my third year of studies, the journey has been nothing short of an exhilarating exploration of both the complexities of code and the rich cultural tapestry of my homeland.
Growing up in Ethiopia, I was captivated by the diverse landscapes that surrounded me. From the bustling streets of Addis Ababa to the serene beauty of the Ethiopian Highlands, each corner of this country seemed to whisper tales of tradition and progress. It was against this backdrop that my passion for technology began to bloom.
My journey into the realm of computer science started as a curious teenager. The allure of creating something tangible out of the digital void, the power to solve real-world problems through lines of code, became a beacon guiding my academic choices. Now, as a third-year student, I find myself diving deeper into the intricacies of algorithms, data structures, and software development.
Outside the realm of academia, I am an avid explorer of my surroundings. Ethiopia, with its rich history and vibrant culture, provides endless opportunities for discovery. Whether wandering through the historic streets of Lalibela or experiencing the rhythmic beats of traditional music in Gondar, I find inspiration in the fusion of the ancient and the contemporary.
